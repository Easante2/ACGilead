IMMIGRATION FEES

Immigration is a complex aspect of law, the U.K. Immigration laws are complex and ever-changing so it is important to seek the advice of an experienced immigration lawyer about your situation. It is for this reason that the solicitors of AC Gilead pride themselves in remaining up to date.

Our job at AC Gilead Solicitors is to understand the details of your situation so we can help you develop a strategy and give you clear advice.

Our services range from application for Entry Clearance to all level of application for Leave to Remain, deportation cases and appeal. Our highly qualified and fully accredited immigration solicitors have the requisite knowledge to handle your case with sensitivity and intensive knowledge. AC Gilead Solicitors offer a wide range of immigration law services including:

  • Naturalisation and citizenship
  • Tier 2 application
  • Sponsorship application for Tier 2 categories
  • Judicial Review
  • Indefinite leave to remain applications
  • EEA/EU Nationals
  • Removal and Deportation
  • Tier 4 applications
  • Bail applications
  • Appeal to the Tribunals/Courts and more
