Our Services

Commercial Law

In AC Gilead, we deal with a range of commercial matters, from the setting up of companies and partnerships to consumer issues and to a far higher degree of specialisation within discrete areas, for example shipping law or the competition aspects of distribution agreements.

Due to the nature of commercial law, we understand the importance of creating a balance between the undertaking of due diligence and the costs. This is why at AC Gilead we can guarantee that the meticulous work in covering every possible risk is proportionate to the price paid. We make it our goal to ensure that the quality of our work is our pride and that is we can confidently help you with the following commercial law issues:

  • Registration of business
  • Agency agreement
  • Intellectual property
  • Franchise agreement
  • Assignment of IP rights
  • Commercial dispute resolution
  • Mediation
