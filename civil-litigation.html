Our Services

Civil Litigation

Litigation matters require meticulous examination of every issue involved as it could escalate out of control. AC Gilead Solicitors will make sure this doesn't happen. We give clear and realistic advice about your chances of success and the likelihood of recovering damages if you are successful. Our job is to get the best result for you in all the circumstances. We always seek to reach agreement through best alternative methods of dispute resolution such as mediation or negotiation. This avoids the expense and unpredictability of a court hearing.

  • County court claims
  • Debt collection for businesses and organisations
  • Insurance disputes
  • Consumer cases
  • Fixed fee ‘Small Claims’ advice
  • Contract disputes and resolution
  • Consumer Credit/Hire Purchase Agreements

Should you need any further assistance in this regard or feel as though you may have a dispute that requires an expert legal opinion to be resolved then please contact us.
